The Valving Curve

Off-the-shelf suspension kits often feature progressive valving, which provides a comfortable street ride but becomes unpredictable at the limit. For track applications and heavy overland rigs, digressive valving offers stiff low-speed damping (reducing body roll during corner entry) while blowing off during high-speed shaft movements (like hitting a curb or washboard dirt roads).

Spring Rate Selection

A high-quality damper is useless if paired with the wrong spring rate. Spring rates must be calculated based on the vehicle's unsprung weight, motion ratio, and intended use case.
