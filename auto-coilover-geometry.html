Suspension 101: Lifting Your Rig Properly

Stop suffering from a bumpy ride. Learn the simple basics of shocks and springs to achieve the perfect balance on and off-road.

Why Cheap Lift Kits Ruin Your Ride

If you just slap cheap "spacer blocks" on your truck to make it look taller, you will ruin how it drives. To do it right, you need proper coilovers (a shock absorber with a spring wrapped around it) designed for the extra weight of your gear.

The Magic of Good Shocks

When you buy a high-quality set of shocks, they do something amazing: they stay stiff when you take a corner on the highway so you don't flip over, but they instantly soften up when you hit a pothole or a dirt road. This gives you a smooth ride no matter where you drive.
