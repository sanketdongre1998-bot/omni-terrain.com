After you place an order

What happens
next.

Live order details will always depend on the product and supplier. These are the basics we want customers to expect from a clear order-support process.

01 / CONFIRM

Order review

We confirm the order information and make sure the important details are clear before fulfillment begins.

02 / PROCESS

Fulfillment

Once a live order is accepted, it is sent into the approved fulfillment process for that product.

03 / TRACK

Shipment update

Tracking information and delivery updates should be shared when they become available for the order.

04 / SUPPORT

Stay available

If something is unclear after ordering, customers can contact OMNI for the appropriate next step.
