Before you buy anything

Four questions
that can save you money.

You do not need to become an expert. Just pause and check these basics before you order a new part, system or accessory.

01
What job do I need this to do?Start with the result you want: safer towing, more power, better navigation or a more useful setup.
02
Will it work with what I already have?Check vehicle details, voltage, space, connector type or other compatibility details before checkout.
03
Do I need more than one part?Some products are part of a larger system. A clear guide should help you spot what else may be needed.
04
Who supports it after I buy?Read the product page and support information. Ask questions when something is unclear.

Recommended gear

Sometimes a guide may point to a product OMNI does not sell directly. In that case, we will tell you clearly that the link goes to another seller.

  • Helpful comparison before an expensive purchase
