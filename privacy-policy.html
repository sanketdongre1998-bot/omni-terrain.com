OMNI Terrain US policy

Privacy Policy

This notice explains how PRP Xpert LLC, operating the OMNI Terrain United States storefront, handles information submitted through product support, the request cart and checkout process.

Effective 24 July 2026US storefrontPRP Xpert LLC

Information collected

We may receive names, email addresses, telephone numbers when supplied, shipping addresses, product selections, order notes, support messages and business correspondence. The website also uses basic browser storage for the request cart.

How information is used

Information is used to answer enquiries, verify products and compatibility, confirm availability and shipping, prepare order terms or invoices, provide support, prevent misuse and maintain business records.

Payments

OMNI Terrain does not ask customers to send card numbers by email. When payment is required, it is collected through a secure payment or invoicing provider identified at that time. Payment providers process information under their own notices.

Sharing and service providers

Information may be shared only as reasonably necessary with hosting, email, payment, supplier, warehouse, carrier, professional-service and fraud-prevention providers, or where required by law. We do not sell personal information to advertisers.

Retention and security

Records are kept only as long as reasonably needed for enquiries, orders, support, accounting, legal or security purposes. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no internet or email system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Contact and requests

Privacy questions or requests may be sent to support@omni-terrain.com. Include enough information for the request to be identified and verified.

The request cart is stored in the customer’s browser using local storage. Clearing browser data removes that local cart. No payment-card data is collected by the current website checkout form.
